Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

The earlier the better, especially for early-morning airport runs and full-day hire. That said, Matt accommodates last-minute and same-day requests whenever his schedule allows — just call directly to check availability.

What are your rates?

Fair, reasonable flat rates quoted up front — no metering, no surge pricing, and no surprises. Submit the reservation form or call for a quote on your specific trip.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards — Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

Do you track flights?

Yes. Every airport pickup is backed by live flight tracking, so your driver is monitoring your arrival and ready the moment you land.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

No problem. Because we track your flight in real time, your pickup is adjusted automatically to your actual arrival — you won't be charged for delays outside your control.

Can I set up recurring or full day rides?

Absolutely. Scheduled commutes and full day chauffeur service are core offerings — set a standing schedule or book Matt for the entire day.
